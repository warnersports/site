Fundraising

WYSA supports approximately 50% of its operations with donations and fundraising. 20% of our budget covers administrative fees (mostly insurance and subscription to TeamSnap). Registration fees cover about half of each sport's costs. The rest is made up by sponsorships, fundraising, and donations.

In addition to paying for uniforms, equipment, league fees, and offials, a portion of our funds goes to scholarships for families who cannot afford full registration fees, especially for our winter ski and snowboard program.

Fundraisers

We hold or have held the following fundraisers:

  • Warner Fall Foliage Festival Lemonade Stand
  • Hero Hustle 5K Run (during Spring into Warner)
  • Snack Shack at home baseball, softball, and soccer games
  • Promotional sales with local businesses
  • Fall soccer tournament

We are always seeking volunteers and additional ideas for fundraisers. If you would like to volunteer, please contact our volunteer coordinator.
