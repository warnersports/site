WYSA is looking for volunteers to replace the following position:

Sponsorship Coordinator

Contact local businesses for sponsorships and donations, purchase banners and signs, and coordinate uniform and t-shirt printing for sports and fundraisers.

Fundraising Director

Plan, coordinate, and run fundraising functions like an annual 5K race, raffles, Fall Foliage Festival lemonade stand, etc.

Volunteer Coordinator

Coordinate volunteers, maintain background check records, purchase snack shack supplies and collect receipts during baseball/softball and soccer season.

Board members serve a two year term, meet monthly for an hour, and fill one or two of the volunteer positions above. Time commitment for volunteer work varies based on the time of year and the number of volunteers involved. You donâ€™t have to be on the board to volunteer!

We also need coaches, assistant coaches, and volunteers to run the snack shack.

As kids grow up, we lose parent volunteers. We need new parents to step up and keep WYSA going.

Contact any board member (email links on the board page) or send an email to contact(at)warnersports.org.

WYSA offers full and partial scholarships for those who cannot afford the full registration fee.

Contact our scholarship coordinator, Glen Moyer, for details.

See our Facebook page for news, photos, and announcements.

Warner Youth Sports Association (WYSA) is a charitable non-profit organization dedicated to giving every child in Warner the opportunity to participate in local sports, regardless of their ability to pay. We are an all-volunteer organization, funded by local business donations, fundraisers, and individual registration fees.
