Announcements

Sunday Soccer Returning!

Sunday Soccer will return to Bagley Field on May 29. This is all-ages, all-community, no-fee pickup for the joy of the game. We're considering a 3pm start time, but email Nathan if you prefer otherwise. We're also considering a time cut-off for younger players so the teens and adults can enjoy some more physical play.

Warner Youth Sports Association (WYSA) is a charitable non-profit organization dedicated to giving every child in Warner the opportunity to participate in local sports, regardless of their ability to pay. We are an all-volunteer organization, funded by local business donations, fundraisers, and individual registration fees.
