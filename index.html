Announcements

Annual Open Board Meeting

Saturday, April 20, 9:30am at Cafe One East

All our encouraged to attend the annual WYSA open board meeting at Cafe One East at 9:30am on Saturday, April 20. This meeting would normally be to vote in new board members. So far two individuals have responded to our recent solicitation but as of yet no one has volunteered for any positions. Free coffee and cookies! Bring your bicycle to have it checked at Bike Check-up at 10am being offered by another organization. And perhaps stay and buy lunch at Cafe One East.

Spring Soccer

After-school soccer will be held at Simonds School four Wednesdays in May (8, 15, 22, and 29; not the 1st). Our provider this year is Caramba Soccer Skills Camp. This is open to Pre-K through 5th graders, and will be $15 per participant. WYSA will provide a chaperone to take attendance at release from school at 2:50pm, and the program will run from 3pm to 4pm. Any questions, or if you're interested in being our new Soccer Director, please contact Nathan Kendrick.

Volunteers Needed

It's time for a new generation to step up to run WYSA.

Our Treasurer and Softball Director will be departing this summer. Our Soccer Director departed last fall. We have been in need of a Vice President, a Volunteer Coordinator, and a Fundraising Coordinator for some time. Our President, Secretary, Social Media Manager, and Baseball Director will be departing soon as our children age out. Our Ski/Snowboard Director would probably like to hand off the reins, too; he's been doing it a while.

If we don't get new volunteers, it will be the end of local youth sports in Warner. Please contact our Board to inquire how you can help keep our organization going for local kids in the years ahead.

Many Thanks to our 2024 Sponsors!

This has been a banner year (pun intended) for sponsorships. In addition to all our baseball and softball teams receiving uniform sponsors, we have received ten advertising banner sponsorships so far this year, with two more expected. See our sponsors page to see all our current and past sponsors, and be sure to thank them and make use of their services.

Warner Youth Sports Association (WYSA) is a charitable non-profit organization dedicated to giving every child in Warner the opportunity to participate in local sports, regardless of their ability to pay. We are an all-volunteer organization, funded by local business donations, fundraisers, and individual registration fees.
