Volunteer at the Fall Foliage Festival

It is a WYSA tradition to volunteer at the festival to empty garbage cans. This is not as bad as it sounds. You are provided with a wagon, bags, etc, and, if you work in pairs, it's easy to get to the cans before they overflow. As thanks for doing this dirty job, WFFF usually donates a few thousand dollars to WYSA, to pay for new equipment and provide scholarships for skiing and other programs.

  1. Go to this link: https://signup.com/go/RMEmyKU
  2. Enter your email address. You do not need to register an account on SignUp.
  3. Scroll down and look for "Trash Removal." Check every day. Click Sign Up and provide your contact information.
  4. Email president@warnersports.org to let me know you signed up so we can report to WFFF how many WYSA members signed up.

Warner Youth Sports Association (WYSA) is a charitable non-profit organization dedicated to giving every child in Warner the opportunity to participate in local sports, regardless of their ability to pay. We are an all-volunteer organization, funded by local business donations, fundraisers, and individual registration fees.
