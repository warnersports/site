Fundraising

Registration fees cover about 65% of each sport's costs. About 15% of our budget covers administrative fees (mostly insurance, payment processing fees, and subscription to TeamSnap and QuickBooks). The remainder is made up by sponsorships, fundraisers, and donations.

In addition to paying for uniforms, equipment, league fees, and officials, about 6% of our funding goes to scholarships for families who cannot afford full registration fees, including our winter ski and snowboard program.

Fundraisers

We hold or have held the following fundraisers:

  • Warner Fall Foliage Festival Lemonade Stand
  • Hero Hustle 5K at Spring into Warner
  • Snack Shack at home games
  • Promotional sales with local businesses
  • Fall soccer tournament

We are always seeking volunteers and additional ideas for fundraisers.

Warner Youth Sports Association (WYSA) is a charitable non-profit organization dedicated to giving every child in Warner the opportunity to participate in local sports, regardless of their ability to pay. We are an all-volunteer organization, funded by local business donations, fundraisers, and individual registration fees.
