Fundraisers

WYSA raises funds by several means to support our programs:

  • Warner Fall Foliage Festival Lemonade Stand
  • Hero Hustle 5K Run
  • Fall Soccer Tournament
  • Raffle
  • Snack Shack

If you would like to assist at a WYSA fundraiser, please contact our volunteer coordinator.

Donations

WYSA pursues donations towards the following programs:

  • Hero Hustle 5K Sponsors - donors' names appear on 5K T-shirt
  • Baseball and Softball team sponsors - donors' names appear on uniforms
  • Ski/Snowboard Scholarships - donations help make program affordable for all families.
  • General/New Equipment - donations towards new goals, balls, and other equipment

If you would like to donate to support WYSA, contact our fundraising director.
