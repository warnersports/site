Warner Youth Sports Association

Sports Offered

Spring

Baseball

March through June.
Ages: 5 to 12.
Registration opens in January.
Teams: T-Ball (5-6yo), Rookies (7-8), Minors (9-10), and Majors (11-12).
Organized through Kearsarge Valley Cal Ripken League.
Registration opens in January.

Softball

March through June.
Ages: 6 to 12.
Teams: U-8, U-10, U-12
Organized through Kearsarge Mountain South.
Registration opens in January.

Spring Soccer Clinic

May
Ages: 4 to 14.
Tuesdays for four weeks after school at Simonds Elementary.
Professional coach and team from New England College.
Pre-K/K and grade-school clinics.
Registration opens in March.

Fall

Soccer

September through October.
Ages: 3 to 12.
Teams: PreK/K, 1/2, 3/4 Boys, 3/4 Girls, 5/6 Boys, 5/6 Girls
Organized through Merrimack Valley Soccer League.
Volunteer coaches.
Registration opens in July.

Winter

Ski/Snowboard Program

December through February.
Ages: 4 to 18.
Organized through WYSA and Pat's Peak.
Buses provided from Simonds, and Kearsarge Middle and High School.
Registration opens in November.
